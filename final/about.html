Hi, I'm Khang, a web development student and multimedia artist based in San Diego, California.

Growing up, I have always considered myself a fine artist at heart, drawing inspiration from pop culture, music, and books, Recently, I find myself drawn more design and development for their emphasis in problem solving and experimentation,. In the beginning of 2024, I made the switch to major in web development.

I’m currently open for commissions and seeking internships to gain industry experience. I try maintain a growth mindset in most aspects of life, and I’m always passionate about learning!
