My stickers

In May 2024, I had the opportunity to become a vendor at a local artist alley. This was how I got a glimpse of what it was like for an independent art business: contacting manufacturer, self-promotion, budgetting, table display, etc. This is the sticker set I made for the event. All stickers are 3x4in, vinyl, and laminated.
